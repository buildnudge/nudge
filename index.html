How Nudge works

01

You tell it everything — once

When you first open Nudge, it asks properly. Your wake time, sleep time, commute, dietary preferences, what's in your kitchen, recurring home tasks, work schedule, how you like to exercise, even how much thinking you want to do before coffee. A proper setup, done once, that makes every day after it genuinely effortless.

02

Every morning, a brief waits for you

Three things. What to wear for the weather, what's for dinner using what's already in your kitchen, and the one logistics task you need to handle today. Ready before you get out of bed.

03

It keeps getting sharper

Every time you swap a suggestion or dismiss a nudge, it learns. The setup gives it a strong foundation — your real behaviour over time makes it feel like it actually knows you.

04

Everything stays on your device

Your schedule, your habits, your kitchen. None of it leaves your phone. Not to train a model. Not to serve you ads. Not ever.
